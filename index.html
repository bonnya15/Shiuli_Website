About Myself

Hi, Shiuli here. I am currently pursuing M.Sc. in Data Science from Chennai Mathematical Institute. I am also a visiting industry project intern at INSEC TEC, Portugal working under the supervision of Dr. Ricardo J Bessa and Dr. Carla Goncalves. My research interest includes a vast horizon of Data Science applications in the domain of Electrical Engineering. I am currently working on the development of a hypothetical data market for renewable energy consumption prediction.


I have completed my B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Durgapur in 2018. After graduation I worked with Jindal Stainless Limited as an Associate Manager at Cold Rolling Mill Electrical and Automation Department for two and a half years.


I am also a singer by passion. I have been learning Indian Classical Music since my childhood. I usually spend my leisure time playing piano, reading story books and observing nature.

CMI Diaries

In 2020, when almost everyone lost their will to the atrocity of pandemic, in one fine afternoon, I got a call letter from Chennai Mathematical Institute. It was one of the most happiest days in my life. Something, which I was dreaming for a while was finally going to be a reality. Due to pandemic, we started late, classes were online and moreover we had to clear three semester exams in a year. We were fortunate enough to get in tough with our professors who tried their best to help us in all the aspects. Here goes the list of some subjects which I enjoyed most at CMI.

  • Programming and Data Structure with Python
  • Probability and Statistics with R
  • Linear Algebra and it's Applications
  • Machine Learning
  • Time Series Analysis
  • Design and Analysis of Algorithms
  • Linear Optimization
